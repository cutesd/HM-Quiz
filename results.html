This quiz was created to help you identify which CHAINS are preventing you from fulfilling your greatest potential with ease and joy. Your full report will reveal more about the specific challenges holding you back. Before you view your results, here is a brief overview of the eight chains.

When you have the chain of SCARCITY, you’re feeling starved for something. It could be time, energy, money, love...anything. In some area of your life, it feels like there’s never quite enough - you’re not living in abundance or power.

When you have the chain of MISTRUST, you’ve got trust issues. Not only do you have issues trusting others, but most importantly you don’t trust yourself. You have a hard time asking for or accepting help from others. As a result, you often feel unsupported and overwhelmed.

When you have the chain of INVISIBILITY, no matter how hard you try, you never feel seen. You may even feel like there’s a veil or hood over your head, hiding you in plain sight. Perhaps in the past when you’ve tried to express yourself, you weren’t accepted or you were criticized, so you went into hiding.

When you have the chain of CONTROL, you have issues letting go. A close cousin of the chain of mistrust, you may find that you take the lead often, and then feel resentful that you’re left doing everything! You feel more secure because you’re in control, but ironically this leaves you feeling isolated and unsupported.

When you have the chain of REPRESSION, you feel silenced, as if you are not able to or allowed to speak your truth. You have trouble speaking up and letting your voice be heard. Ironically if you are repressed, you may also feel invisible. If you don’t know who your true self is, how can he/she be expressed?

When you have the chain of AMBIGUITY, you feel unclear and unable to see what’s in your way. Your perspective has been clouded by fear. When you break this chain, you’re able to reclaim your clear vision, know what’s coming, and see different possibilities.

When you have the chain of DISCONNECTION, you are not connected to the source of your greatest power - your higher self. You’re not accessing your inner guidance: the still small voice within. As a result you often feel powerless or out of control.

When you have the chain of INSIGNIFICANCE, you don’t feel worthy of great things. This may come from your past or from what you were told when you were young. But it keeps you small, always trying to prove your worth and value, without really owning it.

While each of these chains is represented individually, the reality is they all connect to each other. They are self-protective mechanisms that have helped you survive. Each of these chains likely served you at some point, but they must be released so you can thrive. The good news is that when you free yourself of one chain, it immediately starts to loosen or break the others. Breaking these chains is vital if you are going to own your full power.
